About us

Ucard by Ucard Technology

Ucard is a wonderful mobile app for you to share a personalized postcard. Through Ucard, we print your photograph and send it worldwide, with a stamp and postmark from UK, Ireland or China. Then your friends’ can share your “gift” in our Ucard.

About Ucard Technology

Ucard Technology helps customer express love and record-traveling experience with uniquely personalized mobile apps. We have integrated mobile technology, social media and a worldwide printing network to create Ucard, a best-in-class travel postcard app that allows customers to send passional postcards to anywhere in the world in a matter of minutes.

The Ucard Technology team is made of a group of highly talented developers, designers, marketers, analysts and photographer.
